## Pre-compaction memory flush
- **GPU Depreciation Accounting Issue** (2026-03-26): Hyperscalers extended server/GPU useful life from 3-4 years to 5-6 years (2023-2025), collectively reducing ~$18B annual depreciation expense. Amazon reversed to 5 years for AI servers (Nov 2025), citing rapid technology obsolescence.

- **Hyperscaler Depreciation Schedules** (FY2025): Google/Alphabet 6 years (servers/network); Microsoft 2-6 year range (computer equipment); Meta 5.5 years (servers/network, extended Jan 2025); Amazon 5 years AI servers, 6 years networking. Pure-play GPU clouds vary: CoreWeave 6 years, Nebius 4 years, Lambda Labs 5 years.

- **Analyst Concern - Michael Burry Thesis** (Nov 2025): Argues realistic GPU economic life is 2-3 years vs. 5-6 year accounting schedules, potentially inflating hyperscaler earnings by ~20% ($176B understated depreciation 2026-2028). If forced to 3-year schedules, could reduce MSFT/GOOGL 2026-2028 EPS by 15-25%.

- **Key Risk Flag - AI Infrastructure** (2026-03-26): Depreciation policy mismatch with NVIDIA's 18-24 month architecture cadence (Hopper→Blackwell→Rubin). Training clusters refresh 2-3 years; inference 4-5 years. Watch for impairment charges if "value cascade" (training→inference→batch) fails to materialize.
